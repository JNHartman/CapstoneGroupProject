Argent Moom Farms

Owner, Bren Echols

Argent Moon Farm offers beautiful cut flowers and a variety of Alpaca products, including dryer balls, felted soaps, yarn and felted toys. Our Alpacas help us provide a variety of helpful products that we sell at the farmers market. We have reusable eco-friendly dryer balls made entirely from alpaca fiber, which eliminates static, soften clothes and reduce drying time by 15-20%.

Another product we offer are handmade goats milk soaps wrapped in Alpaca fiber, a natural exfoliant which is also great for getting dirt, grime and grease off hands. We have soft, fine Alpaca yarn and needle felted toys, and we plan to sell socks, gloves and scarves during the winter months.
