Groce Produce & Greenhouse

In the spring we start out with flowers and hangings baskets, herbs, Ferns, bedding flowers and vegetable slips. In the summer you'll find many varieties of homegrown tomatoes, squash, zucchini, cucumbers, eggplants, sweet candy onions, sweet and hot banana peppers, bell peppers and more. We also have many varieties of melons. Watermelons, canteloupes, Honeydew, specialty melons such as sensations, canary melons, and sprite melons. Lastly, in the fall we specialize in Mum's, pumpkins and guords, along with our summer produce and winter (cold crops).
