Cherry Farms

Cherry Farms is located in Barren & Allen County Kentucky. We pride ourselves in selling high quality meat. Our beef are born, raised & finished on our farm. It is processed at a USDA processor, so its just like the meat you get in the store, only BETTER​!!!

Besides raising beef cattle, we are a dairy farm and we grow most of our own feed. Not only for the dairy cows, but the beef cows too. They are pasture raised, but are finished on grain, our grain. I also grow lavender and other products that can be used for home decor. I’m currently making all natural personal care items like soap and bath bombs.
