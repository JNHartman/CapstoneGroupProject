Jackson Orchard Nursery

Jackson's Orchard is a local favorite, offering juicy, fresh picked peaches and crisp sweet apples during peak season.

We are typically at the Bounty of the Barrens Farmers Market during peak peach and apple season. We invite people to visit us mid-April to mid-November to enjoy our orchard and purchase in-season fruits, vegetables and spring plants.

Our signature products are our juicy, fresh picked peaches, our crisp, sweet apples & our fresh squeezed apple cider. Our fall Pumpkin Festival is a regional attraction giving families a perfect place to enjoy fall weekends.
